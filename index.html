Our Goal

Operational numerical weather prediction centers have a long history of using hyper-spectral infrared IR satellite observation to improve the weather prediction skills. It is undisputed that impressive progress has been made regardless of the methodological inefficiency. However, the information content of hyper-spectral IR observation is highly rendered by the widely-used channel selection. To improve the data assimilation system’s capability of assimilating full-spectral information content derived from hyper-spectral infrared IR satellite observation, a data assimilation system which takes the advantage of principal component analysis — Heimdallr 2.0 Data Assimilation System, is developed.

Our System

The Heimdallr 2.0 data assimilation system is a 3-Dimensional variational data assimilation system, which enables selected-channel radiance, full-channel radiance, and principal component score assimilation method. This system is capable of assimilating most of the polar-orbiting hyper-spectral IR observation, e.g., CrIS, IASI, HIRAS. It will soon be able to assimilate geostationary hyperspectral IR observation, e.g., GIIRS later this year.
